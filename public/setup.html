📞 Phone Tree Scout

Automatically map any company's phone tree from HubSpot

Step 1 — Install the Bookmarklet

Make sure your bookmark bar is visible (Ctrl+Shift+B in Chrome), then drag this button up to it:

📞 Scout Phone Tree

↑ Drag this to your bookmark bar ↑

Step 2 — Use It

  1. Open any Company record in HubSpot
  2. Click "Scout Phone Tree" in your bookmark bar
  3. Confirm the company name and phone number
  4. Click "Map Phone Tree"
  5. Wait ~2 minutes, then refresh the HubSpot page
  6. The phone tree map appears as a note on the company record

What You'll Get

Scout calls the company, listens to the full phone tree, and uses AI to create a structured map showing:

  1. Fastest path to a human — shortcuts to skip the IVR
  2. All menu options — every "press X for..." option
  3. Key shortcuts — direct extensions, operator codes
  4. Office hours — if mentioned in the recording

Phone Tree Scout — Built for sales teams that hate phone trees.
