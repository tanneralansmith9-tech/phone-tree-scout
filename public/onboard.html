📞 Phone Tree Scout

Automatically map every phone tree in your HubSpot pipeline.

What it does

Scout calls your target companies, listens to their phone trees, and uses AI to create a structured map showing every menu option, the fastest path to a human, shortcuts, and office hours. The map saves directly to the company record in HubSpot.

How to set up

  1. Click the Install button below to connect Scout to your HubSpot
  2. Scout adds a "Scout IVR" tab to your company records
  3. Click "Map Phone Tree" on any company to start mapping
  4. Set up auto-mapping in your workflows for hands-free operation
Install Phone Tree Scout

Requires a HubSpot account. Free to install.

After installing

  1. Go to any company record in HubSpot
  2. Click the "Scout IVR" tab
  3. Click "Map Phone Tree"
  4. Wait ~2 minutes, refresh, and see the structured note on the record
